About Me


My name is Wei Liu. My major in college was Mechanical Engineering. During school, I learned how to finish projects in a team, did a lot of problem solving and had taken some computer Science classes. After graduation, I found what I love to do while working my previous job. My supervisor gave me a project which was automating the validation process by VBA language in excel. Neither my coworker or I had done anything like that before. I tried to break down the problem into small pieces, look things up online, test and try out my thoughts. That is what a developer does! I realized I enjoy writing code and problem solving.That is why I would like to be a developer.
